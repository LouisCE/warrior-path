Juniors (Under 12s)

Our Juniors program is designed to teach children valuable self-defence skills in a safe and fun environment. Classes begin with warm-ups to improve strength, conditioning, and flexibility. Students are taught that social skills and de-escalation are their first line of defence in intimidating situations.

Saturday sessions focus on striking (Boxing & Muay Thai), while Wednesdays focus on grappling (Wrestling & Jiu Jitsu). Every class ends with supervised sparring to help students put what they’ve learned into practice in a safe and supportive way.

Seniors (Ages 12+)

The Senior program dives deeper into the core principles of Mixed Martial Arts (MMA). Warm-ups focus on athletic development, while the curriculum covers practical self-defence applications against a variety of threats, including multiple attackers or weapons.

  • Boxing - Improves punching sharpness, head movement, and footwork
  • Muay Thai - Adds kicks, knees, elbows, and clinch control
  • Wrestling - Teaches takedowns and positional dominance
  • Jiu Jitsu - Focuses on submissions and defending against larger threats

Through this combined system, students become well-rounded martial artists equipped with real-world skills, confidence, and the ability to respond calmly under pressure.
