Striking Arts

Boxing

Boxing is the art of precision striking and defensive movement. You'll learn to throw sharp, accurate punches while mastering footwork, head movement, and upper-body defense. More than just hitting, boxing builds your reaction time, spatial awareness, and ability to stay composed under pressure. It's one of the most effective foundations for stand-up fighting.

Muay Thai

Muay Thai, known as the “Art of Eight Limbs,” brings a full-body approach to striking. You’ll add powerful kicks, knee strikes, elbow combinations, and clinch control to your striking arsenal. This style emphasizes versatility, giving you the tools to become a well-rounded and dangerous striker capable of fighting at all ranges.

Grappling Arts

Wrestling

Wrestling teaches you how to control your opponent through clinches, takedowns, and dominant top positions. It's a vital skill for neutralizing threats and dictating where the fight happens, on the feet or on the ground. You'll build explosive power, balance, timing and relentless pressure, making you a serious threat in close quarters.

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu focuses on neutralising threats and finishing fights with submissions from a variety of positions. You’ll learn to escape bad positions, control the ground, and finish fights with joint locks or chokes. Jiu Jitsu empowers smaller practitioners to outmanoeuvre larger and stronger opponents using technique and leverage.

Why do we combine these?

Together, these disciplines give you the complete toolkit of a true martial artist, blending striking and grappling, strength and technique, calm and aggression. Whether you're training for competition, fitness, or self-defence, you'll be ready for anything.
