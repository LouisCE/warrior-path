About Warrior Path

At Warrior Path, we're more than just a martial arts club. We're a community. Whether you're brand new to martial arts, looking to sharpen your skills, or wanting to compete at regional events, you'll find your place here.

We offer expert coaching combining boxing, Muay Thai, wrestling and BJJ, all taught in a supportive, down-to-earth environment. Our coaches are experienced, our gym is clean and fully equipped, and everyone’s welcome. No egos. No pressure. Just real training.

Seniors (Ages 12+)

Our Seniors program dives deep into the fundamentals of Mixed Martial Arts, blending striking and grappling into a comprehensive system.

Striking classes: Saturday afternoons
Grappling classes: Wednesday evenings

Expect challenging sessions designed to improve strength, agility, and mental toughness in a friendly, respectful environment.

Juniors (Under 12s)

Our Juniors program builds discipline, confidence, and self-defence in a playful and safe setting that kids love.

Striking classes: Saturday afternoons
Grappling classes: Wednesday evenings

All sessions include warm-ups, skill drills, and light supervised sparring to apply what they’ve learned.

Striking Arts

Boxing

Sharpen your hand speed, improve your footwork, and learn how to stay calm under pressure. Boxing builds precision, rhythm, and strategic movement.

Muay Thai

Known as the “Art of Eight Limbs,” Muay Thai adds powerful kicks, knees, elbows, and clinch techniques to your striking arsenal.

Grappling Arts

Wrestling

Develop takedowns, body control, and dominant positions. Wrestling teaches balance, timing, and how to impose your will in close quarters.

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

Learn to neutralize threats and finish fights from any position. Jiu Jitsu empowers smaller practitioners to outmaneuver stronger opponents using technique and leverage.
